What We Offer

Every service.
One team.

Every project starts with a free walkthrough of your property so we can make the right plan for your home.

Service 01

Landscape Up-Lighting

Highlight your trees, columns, and architectural details with carefully aimed fixtures that bring out the best of your home at night.

  • Tree and shrub uplighting
  • Column and pillar accents
  • Architectural facade lighting
  • Color temperature customization

Service 02

Security Lighting

Light up the dark corners of your yard and keep your family safe — without making your home look like a parking lot.

  • Perimeter and corner coverage
  • Motion-activated options available
  • Garage and entry lighting
  • Seamlessly blends with landscape design

Service 03

Patio & Deck Lighting

Turn your outdoor space into somewhere you actually want to spend the evening. Great lighting makes all the difference between a space that gets used and one that doesn't.

  • Pergola and overhead string lighting
  • Step and railing lighting
  • Ambient and accent fixtures
  • Pool and water feature lighting

Service 04

Walkway Lighting

Guide guests to your front door safely and in style. Low-profile fixtures that look great in daylight and create a welcoming glow after dark.

  • Path and border fixtures
  • Step and landing lights
  • Hidden wiring for clean installation
  • Integrated with overall landscape design

Service 05

Driveway Lighting

Make a great first impression from the street and improve visibility when pulling in after dark. The right driveway lighting elevates your home's curb appeal dramatically.

  • Edge and border lighting
  • Entry gate and pillar accents
  • Solar and hardwired options
  • Coordinated with front facade lighting

Service 06

Custom Design

Not sure what you need? No problem — we'll walk your property with you and put together a complete lighting plan that fits your home, your lifestyle, and your budget.

  • Full property consultation at no cost
  • Written lighting design plan provided
  • Phased installation options available
  • No pressure, no obligation
