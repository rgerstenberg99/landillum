Who We Are

We're your neighbors,
not a franchise.

Landscape Illuminations was founded right here in Hoover, Alabama by people who love this community. We started because we kept seeing beautiful homes that went completely unnoticed after dark — and we knew we could change that.

We're not a national franchise with a call center. When you reach out to us, you're talking to the same people who will show up at your door, walk your property with you, and install your lighting by hand.

Our service area covers Greater Birmingham, surrounding communities like Mountain Brook, Vestavia Hills, and Hoover, as well as the Lake Martin area — a region we love and take pride in serving.
